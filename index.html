About Me

Ava Khossravi is a high school senior at Canyon Crest Academy in San Diego, California. Ava was born in Durham, North Carolina and lived there for a few years before moving to San Diego. Ava has an older sister named Emily who also graduated from CCA and is a sophomore in college. In a few months, Ava will move to Boston in order to attend Harvard University. She loves the warm weather in San Diego and will definitely miss it!

Traveling

Ava loves visiting new places. Most recently, Ava visited New York City which is her favorite place to visit in the country. Ava also enjoys traveling to Northern California (specifically San Francisco) where her grandma lives. She is excited about her upcoming trip to Orlando to attend Disney Dreamers Academy, which is an all-expenses paid trip to a major mentorship program for 100 students throughout the United States. There, she looks forward to meeting some of the leaders of Disney including the animator for Lilo and Stitch.

Books

Ava enjoys reading a wide variety of books with her favorite genre being nonfiction. Her favorite book is Outliers by Malcolm Gladwell because it discusses a lot of different factors that make someone successful. Other books by Gladwell that she enjoyed include Blink and Talking to Strangers. In school, she enjoyed reading The Great Gatsby and Frankenstein. She occasionally likes to reread some books that she read when she was younger.
